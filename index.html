About

I specialise in translating theoretical photonics concepts into robust experimental systems. Recent work includes FTIR imaging, LIDAR alignment, infrared laser integration, and on-chip ultralow-loss waveguide design. I build reproducible Python pipelines for simulation and analysis.

Academic

Education & Research

PhD (ECE) — Punjab Engineering College — thesis: ultra-low-loss multispectral on-chip waveguiding architecture. Experience in nano-fabrication and characterization, coursework in quantum optics and inverse design.

Teaching & Outreach

Adjunct Faculty (UpGrad), guest lectures and webinars through OPTICA, SPIE, and IEEE Photonics. Led lab workshops and mentored students on interferometry and photonics CAD tools.
