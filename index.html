About

I specialise in translating theoretical photonics concepts into robust experimental systems. My recent work includes building and debugging FTIR-based imaging systems, LIDAR alignment, infrared laser integration, and on-chip ultralow-loss waveguide design. I enjoy mentoring interns, writing technical proposals, and creating reproducible simulation pipelines with Python.
