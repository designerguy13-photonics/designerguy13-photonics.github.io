About

I specialise in translating theoretical photonics concepts into robust experimental systems. My recent work includes building and debugging FTIR-based imaging systems, LIDAR alignment, infrared laser integration, and on-chip ultralow-loss waveguide design. I enjoy mentoring interns, writing technical proposals, and creating reproducible simulation pipelines with Python.

Academic

PhD (ECE) — Punjab Engineering College — thesis: ultra-low-loss multispectral on-chip waveguiding architecture. Experience in nano-fabrication and characterization, coursework in quantum optics, PI circuits, and inverse design.

Research & Development

Passive FTIR Spectrometer — System Integration

Developed lab and field-capable passive FTIR spectrometer setups, including optical alignment, detector selection (MCT / InSb), and automated data acquisition using LabVIEW.

Phoxonic Crystal Supercell Design

Fresnel Lens Projection Imaging

Selected Projects

100 mm Collimated Source & Interferometric Passive FTIR

Designed a 100 mm collimated source using a 200 mm flat mirror and off-axis parabolic mirror for interferometric imaging in MWIR/LWIR bands.

On-chip Waveguide Optimization using Deep Learning

Worked on inverse design and optimization pipelines for waveguide geometries applying ML techniques to minimize loss and enhance bandwidth.

Development Contract — Imaging FTIR for Vehicle/Tripod Mount

Quoted and designed a compact imaging FTIR for Lynred detector FPAs with specific FOV and optical constraints for field deployment.

Teaching & Mentoring — Intern Projects
